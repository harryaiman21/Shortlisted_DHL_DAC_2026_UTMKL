TITLE: Late Delivery Standard Operating Procedure
SOURCE TYPE: TEXT
TAGS: Delivery, Customer Support, SOP

RAW SOURCE INPUT:
Customer support received repeated messages about delayed parcels during peak delivery period. Agents need a standard way to respond so customers receive consistent updates.

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Check the latest tracking status first.
- Verify the delivery address and contact number.
- Check whether the shipment is delayed at warehouse, linehaul, customs, or last-mile delivery.
- If the delay is more than 24 hours, escalate to the operations team.
- Give the customer a clear next update time.

STRUCTURED SOP CONTENT:
1. Open the shipment record and check the latest tracking status.
2. Verify the customer's delivery address and contact number.
3. Identify the delay point: warehouse, linehaul, customs, or delivery route.
4. If the parcel is delayed more than 24 hours, raise an escalation to operations.
5. Record the escalation reference number in the customer case.
6. Inform the customer of the latest status and expected follow-up time.
7. Close the case only after the customer has received an update or the parcel is delivered.
