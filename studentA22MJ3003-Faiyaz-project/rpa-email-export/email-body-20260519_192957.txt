Subject: DHL KB Source Submission - Delivery Address Change SOP

Hi Digital Automation Center Team, Please convert the following operational
notes into a Knowledge Base article. During shipment processing, customers
sometimes request a delivery address change after the parcel has already
been created in the system. The support team must first verify that the
shipment is not out for delivery. If the parcel is still at the facility or
in transit, the agent should confirm the customer's identity, check whether
the new address is within the same service area, and then raise an address
amendment request. If the parcel is already out for delivery, the agent
should not promise same-day redirection. The customer should be informed
that the courier may attempt the original address first, and the
redirection will be handled in the next available delivery cycle. Please
include these points in the SOP:

   - Verify shipment status before making any promise.
   - Confirm customer identity and shipment ownership.
   - Check whether the new address is serviceable.
   - Create an address amendment request.
   - Notify warehouse or delivery operations if manual handling is needed.
   - Update the customer with the expected next action.
